Welcome to the MathSci Alliance, a dynamic community dedicated to fostering collaboration and innovation in the fields of mathematics and science. Our association brings together mathematicians and scientists from diverse backgrounds to create a dynamic network of expertise and knowledge sharing. At MathSci, we believe in the power of interdisciplinary collaboration to drive innovation and progress. Our members engage in a wide range of activities, from collaborative research projects to organizing conferences and workshops that promote learning and networking opportunities.
Our mission is to support and empower mathematicians and scientists at every stage of their careers, from aspiring students to seasoned professionals. Through our initiatives, resources and events, we aim to inspire creativity, promote excellence and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in mathematics and science globally.
Join us in shaping the future of math and science where collaboration and innovation thrive!

ABOUT US

Empowering Excellence, Fostering Innovation, and Driving Global Progress in Mathematics and Science

The Association of Mathematicians and Scientists (AMS) is the leading professional organization dedicated to promoting excellence and collaboration in the fields of mathematics and science. Our diverse community includes researchers, educators, students and industry professionals, all united by a passion for innovation and discovery. At AMS, our mission is to facilitate interdisciplinary exchange, support professional development, and lead effective research initiatives. We provide a platform for networking, knowledge sharing and mentoring, and foster a culture of collaboration and continuous learning. Our activities range from organizing conferences, workshops and seminars to publishing cutting-edge research and educational resources. We also advocate for the importance of math and science in addressing global challenges and advancing society. Join us in shaping the future of math and science where expertise meets progress in innovation and collaboration.
